Our History

SalamX Limited was founded as a parent company for innovative platforms rooted in Qur’an and Hadith principles. Its first major project, SalamChat (SChat), promotes safe and respectful communication online. SalamX balances modern technology with spiritual guidance, ensuring honesty, fairness, and integrity in every solution.

About SalamX Limited

We are a CAC-registered technology company based in Sokoto, Nigeria, led by Umar Bello Tafida, CCNA, ACA — Co-founder and CEO. Our vision is to build Africa’s leading digital ecosystem, creating transformative and ethical technology grounded in Islamic values.
